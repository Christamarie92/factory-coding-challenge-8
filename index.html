Our Philosophy

We Are What We Eat

The Veggie Cafe's natural foods bring together vegans, vegetarians, omnivores, and foodies alike. At Viva Vegan, we believe that food should make the body and mind feel complete, inside and out. Our commitment to natural foods and the environment runs deep; our packaging is eco-friendly and won’t harm the Earth.

Our diverse menu is created with natural ingredients that balance the whole body including our juices, smoothies and elixirs to the incredibly delicious vegan, wheat free and gluten free treats and cakes are baked fresh each morning.

We are involved in the community and support the use of non-GMOs. We are committed to providing fresh, natural, non-GMO food to the community. We use biodegradable take-out containers, bags and utensils. Our tables are made of reclaimed wood and we also use all natural cleaners.

Our History

Our Vision

The restaurant, formerly known as Goldie Treats, is deeply focused on health, wellness, nutrition, and last but not least, great flavors. We prepare delicious, fresh, vegan foods and pastries daily. Several menu items change daily, ensuring that Viva Vegan has something exciting to offer every day

Our History

Goldie Treats opened its doors in the spring of 2009 with Brittany Fawn, as the executive chef who had previously worked with Real Food Daily for six years. Many of her recipes are still being utilized throughout our menu. In 2019, we renovated the space and rebranded as The Veggie Cafe.
